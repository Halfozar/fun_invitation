Nikkah Countdown 💑

Arikuthicheral

Details :

Arikuthal Kalyanam (അരികുത്തൽ കല്യാണം) is a cherished pre-wedding ritual practiced in parts of the Malabar region of Kerala, especially among Mappila Muslim families.

➜

Gulabi

Details :

Gulabi Function (Gulabi Ceremony) is a joyful pre-wedding ritual. The event is festive and filled with laughter, music and togetherness. A pink paste is applied on the bride by her loved ones.

➜

Glitz

Details :

Glitz is a dazzling disco-themed pre-wedding celebration that brings together family and friends for an evening of music, dance, games and entertainment. With glittering décor, sparkling lights and a dress code featuring shimmery and sequined outfits, the event creates a lively atmosphere where everyone celebrates the couple, strengthens family bonds and makes unforgettable memories before the wedding festivities begin.
